Add the first CodeGraphy Particles plugin with graph background particle presets.

When the plugin is enabled, it adds a Particles section to the Theme popup and lets users toggle Synapse, Rain, Constellations, Perlin Flow, Leaves, Sparkles, Embers, Snow, and workspace custom TypeScript effects behind the Relationship Graph. The plugin owns the particle controls, canvas renderer, prewarmed animations, green Leaves preset, TypeScript custom effect compilation from `.codegraphy/particles/`, a layered Snow preset with drifting depth and crystalline flakes, and a Fireflies custom effect example with wandering glow, trails, pulses, and flashes under `examples/.codegraphy/particles/`.
